Visit Saratoga

This enclave of Silicon Valley is also home to a
world-class Japanese garden and center for the arts

Top three activites to do at Saratoga

Hakone Gardens

There are several buildings that you can get to and around, a bamboo garden, a small pond with koi and a rock garden. Please get the brochure that has the highlights of the gardens and the story behind them.

Skyline to the Sea Trail

The trail is beautiful and well marked. And hiking under the canopy of the redwoods makes it a comfortable hike, even on hot days. Watch your step as there are often banana slugs on the trail. And the 3 waterfalls on the Berry Creek Falls spur trail are absolutely beautiful and not to be missed.

Montalvo Arts Center

Montalvo has something for everyone. A hike in the woods, indoor and outdoor concerts, a relaxing and beautiful garden, art exhibits, classes, a historic mansion and more. Villa Montalvo is well worth a visit.
